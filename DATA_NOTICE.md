# Data notice

This repository contains a structured extraction of selected nutrition table pages from a government publication:
- **Tabel Komposisi Pangan Indonesia (2020)**
- Cover/publisher statement in the source PDF: **Kementerian Kesehatan**

## Legal positioning

This repository does **not** claim that the underlying source data is under a formal open-data license.

The legal basis most relevant to public access appears to be **UU No. 28 Tahun 2014 tentang Hak Cipta**:
- Official page: `https://peraturan.go.id/id/uu-no-28-tahun-2014`
- Official PDF: `https://peraturan.go.id/files/uu28-2014bt.pdf`

Most relevant provisions read:
- **Pasal 43(b)**: acts of publication/distribution/communication/reproduction of material carried out by or on behalf of the government are not treated as copyright infringement **unless** protection is expressly stated by law, by a statement on the work, or at publication time.
- **Pasal 44(1)**: reproduction/use/adaptation with complete source citation for education, research, scientific writing, reporting, criticism, or review is not treated as infringement if it does not unreasonably harm the copyright holder.

## Important caution
- **Pasal 42** is not the main basis here; it applies to things like statutes, official speeches, court decisions, and similar materials.
- This repository therefore relies more cautiously on the logic of **Pasal 43(b)** and **Pasal 44(1)**.
- In the source PDF pages reviewed for publication context (`1-14`), no explicit copyright notice, open license, or copying prohibition was found. That helps, but it is **not equivalent to a formal open license**.

Use this repository with clear attribution to the original government source.
